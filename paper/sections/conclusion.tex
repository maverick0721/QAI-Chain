\section{Conclusion}

The stack stays blunt on purpose: PPO learns, uncertainty routes, shield clamps, deterministic branch stays trustworthy. Under shift that bundle shifts failure shapes versus executing the policy naked, and the learned block can stay small.

VQC-style hardware in the text is only a cheap nonlinear uncertainty probe under classical simulation. On-chain logging is optional audit glue. Splitting those roles keeps casual readers from misreading ``quantum'' as a throughput brag.

Evidence covers the thirty-seed Safety Gym pair, fifty-seed governance controller tables, calibration and perturbation diagnostics, plus the cost appendix. The reproducibility block lists the scripts that rebuilt the whole trail from logs.
