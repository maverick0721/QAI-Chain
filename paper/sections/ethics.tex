\section{Ethics and Broader Impacts}

Deployment risk is concentrated in incorrect autonomy under adversarial conditions: a policy can optimize short-term reward while violating safety constraints or introducing hard-to-detect governance drift. The mitigation used here is layered control (uncertainty-gated fallback, shielded execution, and auditable records), but this does not replace formal security review, incident response planning, or domain-specific regulatory checks before any production use.
